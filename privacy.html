Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZenAutomation ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website, contact us, or use our products and services. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, phone number, and company name when you submit a form or request a demo.
  • The content of your messages and any information you choose to share with us.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data such as pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, and general interaction patterns.
  • Device and technical data such as browser type, operating system, and approximate location derived from your IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the products, services, or demos you request.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and offerings.
  • To send relevant updates, proposals, or marketing communications (which you can opt out of at any time).
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or misuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how our website is used. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; some features may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business (such as hosting, analytics, and communication tools), strictly to the extent needed to perform their services. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at info.zenhubautomation@gmail.com, or write to us in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
